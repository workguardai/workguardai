How It Works

From DWG file
to live insight

Upload your construction plan once — WorkGuardAi reads it mathematically, maps your site, and tracks real progress automatically.

01

Upload your DWG plan

Drop your architectural or civil engineering file. WorkGuardAi parses and digitally renders your full excavation and site layout.

02

AI maps plan vs. reality

Our model continuously compares live site progress against your uploaded plan — zero manual data entry from managers.

03

Smart AI notifications fire

When milestones are reached or something falls off-track, architects and managers are alerted instantly across all sites.

04

Monitor multiple sites at once

One dashboard, all your sites, real-time progress. No spreadsheets, no status calls, no guesswork.

Our Story

Built from the
groundwork up

WorkGuardAi was founded in October 2023 by Endrit Kola with a simple observation: the construction industry — one of the world's largest — was still managing multi-million euro projects with spreadsheets, phone calls, and manual site visits.

With a background spanning commercial sales, international startups, digital products, and the EMEA telecoms region, Endrit set out to build an AI monitoring platform that removes the manual burden from site managers entirely — giving infrastructure businesses real-time visibility from a single dashboard.

Supported by LUT University, Helsinki Incubators, the University of Helsinki, and Interreg EU funding, WorkGuardAi has grown from an idea into a working MVP with its first pilot project underway with ANK Construction Company.
